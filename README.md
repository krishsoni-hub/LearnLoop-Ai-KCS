# LearnLoop AI — Where Knowledge Becomes Currency

LearnLoop is a peer-to-peer knowledge exchange ecosystem where students teach and learn. It is powered by a gamified **Knowledge Credit** economy that turns passive consumers into active contributors. 

This repository houses the source code and documentation for the LearnLoop AI web application.

---

## 🎯 The Problems We Solve
Traditional learning and tutoring systems are broken. LearnLoop addresses these critical barriers:
- **Affordability**: Platforms like Chegg or Wyzant charge money, excluding millions. LearnLoop relies on a self-sustaining credit economy.
- **Time Constraints**: Students need help at odd hours (e.g., 2 AM before an exam). Peer networks make 24/7 help possible.
- **Trust & Approachability**: Students often hesitate to ask teachers; peer interaction feels safer and more approachable.
- **Incentive Gap**: Excellent students have no compelling reason to teach others. We provide a robust reward structure.
- **Unstructured Learning**: WhatsApp and Discord are chaotic. LearnLoop offers structured, verified learning.
- **Knowledge Wastage**: Subject experts graduate, and their knowledge evaporates. We keep that knowledge in the loop!

---

## 🚀 Key Innovations
Our platform brings breakthrough features that set it apart from any traditional tutoring service:
1. **AI Session Summarizer**: Auto-generates session notes and sends them to both participants after each session.
2. **Concept Graph**: Visualizes how topics connect across sessions, showing students their complete learning trajectory.
3. **Skill DNA Profile**: A dynamic radar chart of subject strengths built from session history—your academic fingerprint.
4. **Smart Credit Pricing**: AI dynamically adjusts credit cost based on demand/supply for a topic (surge pricing for knowledge).
5. **Doubt Clustering**: Groups similar doubts and serves one tutor to multiple students simultaneously for massive efficiency.
6. **AI Doubt Pre-Processor**: Before posting, AI reformulates vague questions into clear, answerable ones to raise question quality.

## 🧠 Powered by AI
Seven interconnected AI systems work together to create the smartest learning platform ever built, including:
- **Tutor-Learner Matching**: NLP + collaborative filtering to match users based on expertise, language, and past ratings.
- **Doubt Processing**: NLP to understand and route questions effectively.
- **Session Intelligence**: AI actively monitors and summarizes the quality of peer-to-peer sessions.

## 🔄 How It Works (The 6 Steps to Knowledge)
1. **Registration & Verification**: Sign up with an institution email -> OTP verification -> create a profile (expertise + interests) -> receive **20 starter credits**.
2. **Post a Help Request**: Fill out a request form -> AI auto-tags subject/topic -> set urgency + language + duration -> request goes live on the feed.
3. **AI Matching**: The system scores all tutors (using expertise, availability, rating, and history) -> recommends the top 3 -> accept or browse all.
4. **Session Execution**: Enter the session room -> real-time chat + collaborative whiteboard + optional video (WebRTC) -> tutor shares notes.
5. **Credit Transfer & Feedback**: Session ends -> learner rates tutor -> AI scores quality -> credits transfer -> badges & streaks update.
6. **Profile Growth**: Credits, badges, and subject mastery update -> leaderboard refreshes -> AI-generated session summary is sent to your inbox.

## 👥 User Roles
Everyone in LearnLoop has a role, and roles are completely fluid:
- **Learner**: Posts help requests, spends credits, and rates sessions.
- **Tutor**: Accepts requests, conducts sessions, earns credits, and builds reputation.
- **Hybrid (Most Users)**: Every student is both a learner and a tutor—this is the core philosophy of LearnLoop!
