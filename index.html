The Problem

Learning Is Broken

Traditional tutoring is expensive, teacher-centric, and unavailable for quick, topic-specific doubts. Students who excel have no meaningful platform to share their expertise.

Affordability Barrier

Platforms like Chegg or Wyzant charge money, excluding millions of students from quality help.

Time Barrier

Students need help at odd hours — 2 AM before an exam — not during business hours.

Trust Barrier

Students hesitate to ask teachers; peer interaction feels safer and more approachable.

Incentive Gap

Students who know subjects have no compelling reason to teach others — no reward structure.

Unstructured Learning

WhatsApp groups and Discord servers are chaotic and unverified — no quality control.

Knowledge Wastage

Every batch produces hundreds of subject experts whose knowledge evaporates after graduation.

What is LearnLoop?

LearnLoop is an AI-powered platform that connects students who need help with peers who can teach — all powered by a cashless Knowledge Credit economy. No money, no barriers — just pure knowledge exchange.

How Credits Work

Earn credits by teaching what you know. Spend them to learn what you don't. Every session transfers credits from learner to tutor — creating a self-sustaining economy where teaching is rewarded and learning is accessible.

AI-Powered Matching

Our AI engine analyzes subject expertise, ratings, language, and learning style to find your perfect tutor match in seconds. GPT-powered doubt reformulation ensures your questions are clear and structured.

Live Session Workspace

A real-time collaborative workspace with whiteboard, chat, and video. Teach and learn together with drawing tools, file sharing, and an AI-generated session summary at the end.

Gamification & Growth

Earn badges, maintain teaching streaks, and climb the campus leaderboard. Every session builds your Skill DNA — a radar chart showing your expertise growth across subjects.

Verified & Secure

Only real, institution-verified students can join. Email OTP verification, content moderation, and AI-powered review analysis maintain a trusted and safe learning community.

How It Works

Six Steps to Knowledge

From signup to mastery — here's the complete LearnLoop journey.

1

Registration & Verification

Sign up with institution email → OTP verification → create profile (expertise + interests) → receive 20 starter credits

2

Post a Help Request

Fill request form → AI auto-tags subject/topic → set urgency + language + duration → request goes live on feed

3

AI Matching

System scores all tutors using expertise, availability, rating, language, and history → top 3 recommended → accept or browse all

4

Session Execution

Enter session room → real-time chat + collaborative whiteboard + optional video (WebRTC) → tutor shares notes → timer runs

5

Credit Transfer & Feedback

Session ends → learner rates tutor (1-5 stars + text) → AI scores quality → credits transfer → badges & streaks update

6

Profile Growth

Credits, badges, subject mastery, and reputation rank update → leaderboard refreshes → AI-generated session summary sent to inbox
