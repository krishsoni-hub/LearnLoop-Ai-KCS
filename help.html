Help Center

Credits & Payments

What are the exact ways to earn Knowledge Credits?
You can earn credits by teaching a session (+10-30), sharing a learning resource (+2), completing your profile (+5), or hitting your first teaching streak (+10).

How are credit costs calculated for a help request?
The cost is auto-calculated based on the urgency of the request and the intended duration of the session; longer and more urgent requests require more credits.

What happens if I run out of credits?
If your balance is insufficient, the credit preview will turn red. You can earn more by teaching others or browsing existing requests that match your expertise.

Tutors & Matching

How does the matching system decide which tutor is "Best"?
The platform uses a scoring algorithm based on expertise match (40%), rating (25%), language (20%), availability (10%), and teaching style (5%).

Can I see why a specific tutor was recommended to me?
Yes, each recommended tutor card includes an AI-generated explanation detailing why they are a good match for your specific request.

What if no tutors are available when I post a request?
The app will notify you when someone becomes available, or you can choose to convert your request into a scheduled session for later.

Live Sessions & Profile

What tools are available during a live session?
You have access to a collaborative whiteboard with drawing tools (pen, shapes, etc.), a real-time chat, and the ability to upload resources like PDFs and images.

Can I save a summary of my learning session?
Yes, after a session ends, an AI-generated summary of the key topics covered is provided to both the learner and the tutor.

How is my "Skill DNA" calculated on my profile?
The radar chart is based on the subject expertise you declared during registration and updated using mastery scores earned from the sessions you have taught.

What is a "Streak Freeze"?
A Streak Freeze allows you to maintain your daily teaching activity record if you miss a day; you can earn one freeze per week.

Contact Support
